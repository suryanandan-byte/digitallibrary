About Us

A digital library is a managed collection of information and resources stored in digital formats and accessible over a network, typically the internet. Unlike a traditional library with physical books and materials, a digital library's holdings—such as e-books, articles, journals, images, audio files, and videos are available 24/7 from anywhere in the world.
